UNIQUE SCRIPT CONDITIONS


SMILE, for endless customization.


Reporting on a speeding vehicle is one thing, but what if you want to be notified of speeding in a particular zone, at a particular time or under a particular set of conditions. The Ctrack SMILE script allows Crystal users the flexibility to setup unique conditions and scenarios, which will activate a report or initiate a predetermined action, if those conditions are met or exceeded. You can even combine multiple conditions (up to 32 different queries) under the same scenario. Reduce the speed of your trucks on gravel roads, (lowering your maintenance and repair costs), limit the loading or unloading of goods and cargo to specific locations; and just about any operating condition in any industry that you can think of. It’s 35 years of Ctrack research and development expertise, offering you ultimate customization and the Power to Predict.

SOS PANIC BUTTON


Always on call, always by your side.


The welfare of vulnerable workers a priority for any business. Their wellbeing and safety has to be given plenty of consideration and appropriate measures and processes need to be put in place. Ctrack offers panic buttons for remote employees, giving the freedom and peace of mind to operate in locations with the notion that help is always on standby, all the time. LOAD AND ASSET MONITORING


Load up on info.


If you’re in the business of transportation, you’ll know that there’s a lot more to fleet management, than the position of the truck and trailer. You’ll need full visibility of everything, and that includes what’s being loaded in the back. Using RFID technology, our powerfully simple Crystal software is able to track that the correct goods are on the correct truck, headed to the correct destination. Even after arrival, trust Crystal to give you real time info about which cargo have been offloaded where, with control measures in place to secure anything out of the ordinary from happening, ahead of time. Using our advanced SMILE Script, you can setup specific conditions that will allow or prohibit the offloading of goods and packages. Keep back the back doors of delivery vans or trailers locked, if your cargo isn’t at the right location at the right time, with real-time alerts so that you can act, before you have to react. It’s peace of mind and confidence from the Power to Predict, for you and your partners which not only reduces risk, but lowers insurance costs and limits losses as well.
