FLEXIBLE SOFTWARE PLATFORM

Unlock functionality to suite your exact requirements.

Kick the day off with Job Management, assigning tasks and planning your routes using the Location Functionality, for the most efficient way to get from A to B. Use the Alerts Function to keep the right people informed ahead of the time, all the time. If the day does take an unexpected turn, you can rely on the Snapshots Summary to get an instant view in real time, of what’s really happening, right now.

Data is packaged and visualized in the simplest format possible, for the kind of reporting and insights to make informed decisions on the fly. Use Replays to retrace your steps back in time along a route or even a sequence of events in the day, so that you always have 100% clarity on what happened, when, where and why – and how to avoid it next time. Check-in on the progress of people and your property with the Trip Summary Reports. Pick up the slack, optimize or act before you are forced to react. And when the day is done, generate reports at the touch of button which are instant, interactive and intuitive.

JOB SCHEDULING


Schedule, task and optimize on the fly.


Pull all the moving parts together into a crystal clear plan with Crystal Jobs Scheduling. Here, you have the ability schedule and manage multiple tasks across the business, assigning custom notifications to all the relevant parties in the mix. You can load your tasks by the asset itself or by the person operating it, and it doesn’t matter if it’s a one-time activity or a re-occurring task; just schedule, assign and go! Once you’ve effortlessly scheduled what needs to go where, and when it needs to be there, sit back and let Crystal do the rest. Schedules can be saved as routes, and you can share these with other users to track what’s happening in real time, against the plan for the day.
